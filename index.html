Klaipeda is a city located on the West coast of Lithuania; just 4 hours away from Vilnius (the capital). This city attracts more than 17.000 tourists annually, combining the fresh air from the pine forest and the salty wind from the side of the Baltic sea. Moreover, Klaipeda has the biggest industrial port in the region, accepting both tankers and cruise ships. Choosing Free Walking Tours, Klaipeda you will have an opportunity to dive into this diverse city, and to experience the contrast between the romantic Old Town and the fast pace of port. We offer two types of tours: Day and Night, which cover different aspects of life in Klaipeda. They last for 1.5 hours each, and take place on Tuesdays and Fridays.
